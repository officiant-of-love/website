Officiant of Love is the premier Los Angeles Multi-Denominational Minister

Helena, the Officiant of Love, is dedicated to creating beautiful moments for any event in your life. Coming from a multi-faith background, Helena specializes in compassionate events celebrating all aspects of faith and togetherness. She has been featured in Martha Stewart Wedding, Style Me Pretty Magazines and the Carats and Cake blog.
